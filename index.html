Cafes and Museums Galore

This map is intended to show parks in Southwestern Yuma that are close to the Marine Corps Air Station (MCAS Yuma). All parks are surrounded by a one mile radius to show proximity. This map also shows that all parks on base are within walking distance of base housing.

Which parks have the most trails?

The park that actually has the most trails in Yuma is an unnamed park with 5.8 miles of trails throughout. Following this is the West Wetlands Park and the Gateway Park with around 4.5/4/75 miles of parks, these are located on the Colorado River, which lies to the North of this map. All other parks in Yuma have 1 mile or less of trails to explore.
