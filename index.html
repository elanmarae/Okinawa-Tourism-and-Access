Cafes and Museums Galore

First time in Okinawa? How about stopping in for some CoCo's Curry or tea at the Cat Cafe? Maybe an afternoon at an Art Museum, or perhaps a trip to one of the outlying islands by ferry? You can get there from here. This map shows a majority of Naha City and all the amenities you can access just by public transportation!

Friendly Ammenities!

As you can see in the pop-out map, the tourist attractions in Okinawa are very accessible! All of the cafes and museums in Naha fall within 1/4 kilometer of a bus stop or railway stop. And don't forget the ATM's. Many establishments in Okinawa prefer cash in hand over card transactions. Luckily the ATMs are also readily available within 1/4 kilometer of public transportation stops. So grab some Yen before you grab your coffee!
